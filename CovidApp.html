Overview

We were given a design challenge to redesign the COVID Vaccination booking experience for Ontarinians as part of a course assignment, after conducting a series of user research methodologies, we decided to create an integrated booking system to allow Ontarians to easily book and manage their COVID vaccination appointments.

Problem

The vaccination booking experience has been challenging for many Ontarians due to:

Fragmented Reservation Platforms
From provincial to municipal, each public authority offers their own booking systems that overwhelms citizens and causes overlapped reservations being made.

A Lack of Usability
Currently, reserving a vaccination appointment can only be done on website where users need to enter excessive information in order to get their appointment. If the user hopes to reschedule or cancel the appointment, they have to login to a seperate portal than the one they used to book their vaccination.

Solution

We designed an integrated booking system to make it simple for Ontarians to schedule and manage their COVID vaccination appointments all at once.

Design Process

Define: User Flow

During the design process, we further revised our user goals down to 3 mostly needed ones as our user research reveals the rest 2 goals were hardly conducted or even noticed by target.
User Goal 1: Book a new vaccination appointment. User Goal 2: Modify existing appointments.

Ideate: Style Guide

We chose colors with low saturations to bring an overall calming impression to our users.

Prototype

There are 6 key features we developed during the prototyping phases:
1. Mandatory Account Registration
2. Integrated Home Screen
3. Dual Booking Options
4. Clear Confirmation Screen
5. Freedom to Make Changes
6. Integrated Appointment Management
